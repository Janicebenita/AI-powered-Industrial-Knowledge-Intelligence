# Pitch Deck Content

## Slide 1: Industrial Brain AI

Unified Asset & Operations Intelligence Platform.

## Slide 2: Problem

Industrial knowledge is trapped across manuals, SOPs, work orders, inspection reports, spreadsheets, drawings, audit records, and emails. Teams waste hours searching, miss repeated failure patterns, and make compliance decisions without complete evidence.

## Slide 3: Solution

Industrial Brain AI ingests plant documents, extracts entities, builds an asset knowledge graph, and powers a cited AI copilot for maintenance, RCA, compliance, and executive insights.

## Slide 4: Demo

Ask: `Why has Pump P101 failed repeatedly?`

The system answers with citations, confidence, related assets, related documents, and preventive actions.

## Slide 5: Architecture

Document ingestion → OCR → extraction → chunking → embeddings → vector DB → knowledge graph → agents → cited copilot.

## Slide 6: Differentiation

- Evidence-first answers
- Industrial entity extraction
- Asset-centered graph
- Compliance gap detection
- RCA report export
- Multi-agent operational intelligence

## Slide 7: Business Value

Lower downtime, faster audits, less duplicated engineering work, better institutional memory, and faster troubleshooting.

## Slide 8: Roadmap

Plant integrations, CMMS connectors, IoT historian, ERP, EAM, advanced vision for P&IDs, Neo4j at scale, SSO, and predictive risk models.
