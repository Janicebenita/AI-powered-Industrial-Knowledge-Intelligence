# Business ROI Calculation

Example plant assumptions:

- 120 maintenance engineers and technicians
- 45 minutes per person per day searching for historical documents
- Average loaded cost: $55/hour
- 250 working days per year
- Search reduction from Industrial Brain AI: 35%

Annual search cost:

`120 * 0.75 * 55 * 250 = $1,237,500`

Estimated annual productivity recovery:

`$1,237,500 * 35% = $433,125`

Additional value areas:

- Avoided downtime from faster RCA
- Reduced audit preparation time
- Better preventive maintenance planning
- Lower compliance risk
- Faster onboarding of new engineers
