# Risk and Future Roadmap

## Risks

- OCR quality for poor scans.
- Entity extraction variation across plants.
- Document permission leakage if retrieval filters are not enforced.
- Over-reliance on AI summaries without field validation.

## Mitigations

- Human-in-the-loop entity review.
- Confidence thresholds and citation requirements.
- Role-based and document-level retrieval controls.
- Audit logs for every answer and source citation.

## Roadmap

1. CMMS/EAM connectors: SAP PM, Maximo, Infor.
2. Historian integrations: PI System, SCADA, DCS alarms.
3. Neo4j graph backend for enterprise-scale graph traversal.
4. pgvector and hybrid retrieval.
5. P&ID vision extraction.
6. Mobile technician mode.
7. SSO and tenant isolation.
8. Predictive maintenance risk models.
