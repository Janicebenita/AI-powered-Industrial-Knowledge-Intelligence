# Demo Script

## Opening

Industrial Brain AI is a unified asset and operations intelligence platform. It turns fragmented plant knowledge into cited, graph-connected, AI-powered decisions for maintenance, reliability, operations, safety, quality, compliance, and leadership.

## Script

1. Show Landing Page.
   - Message: Industrial Brain AI transforms industrial knowledge into operational intelligence.
   - Click Launch Platform.

2. Show Executive Dashboard.
   - Documents are already seeded.
   - Entity extraction and graph counts are visible.
   - High-risk assets and repeated failure patterns are visible.

3. Show Ingestion.
   - Explain supported files: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, CSV, PNG, JPG, TIFF, scanned reports, SOPs, work orders, incidents, regulatory checklists, and engineering metadata.
   - Upload an extra text or CSV document if desired.

4. Show Entities.
   - Point out equipment tags, work orders, inspection findings, procedures, regulations, spare parts, hazards, dates, roles, and locations.

5. Show Graph.
   - Explain relationships such as `ASSET_FAILED_WITH`, `PROCEDURE_REFERENCES_ASSET`, `INSPECTION_FOUND`, `REGULATION_APPLIES_TO`, and `SPARE_PART_USED_IN`.

6. Show Copilot.
   - Ask: `Why has Pump P101 failed repeatedly?`
   - Emphasize citations, confidence, related assets, documents, and recommended next actions.

7. Show Asset 360.
   - Select `P-101`.
   - Show failures, work orders, inspections, documents, risk score, and risk drivers.

8. Show RCA.
   - Show draft RCA report for `P-101`.
   - Explain likely root causes: misalignment, low suction pressure, cavitation, and strainer fouling.
   - Export the RCA PDF report.

9. Show Compliance.
   - Show uncovered `API-510` and `NFPA-70E` evidence gaps.
   - Generate audit package summary.

10. Show Lessons.
   - Show the multi-agent findings from document intelligence, graph, maintenance, compliance, RCA, lessons learned, and executive insights.

11. Show Metrics.
   - Explain extraction precision and recall estimates, retrieval quality, citation coverage, gaps, and repeated patterns.

## Closing

This is not a generic chatbot. It is an evidence-first industrial intelligence system. It builds an asset knowledge graph, refuses unsafe uncited answers, detects repeated failures and compliance gaps, and generates auditable operational intelligence.
