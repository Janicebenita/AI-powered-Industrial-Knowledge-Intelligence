# LOTO Procedure
Procedure ID: LOTO-PLANT-A-001
Applies to: P101, C201, EP501, B203

Purpose:
Prevent unexpected energization, startup, or release of stored energy during maintenance.

Steps:
Notify affected operators. Shut down equipment. Isolate electrical, mechanical, pneumatic, hydraulic, and process energy sources. Apply lockout tagout devices. Verify zero energy state. Document isolation points and personnel roles.

EP501 electrical panel:
Arc flash boundary and NFPA-70E controls must be reviewed before energized diagnostic work.

P101 pump maintenance:
Motor starter isolation, valve isolation, drain verification, and zero pressure confirmation are mandatory before opening casing.
