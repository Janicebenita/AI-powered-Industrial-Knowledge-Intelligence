# Engineering Notes P101
Asset: P101 Condensate Transfer Pump
OEM: FlowServe

Engineering review:
Pump P101 has operated near the left side of its curve during low demand periods. This can increase recirculation, vibration, seal face instability, and cavitation risk.

Observed pattern:
Repeated seal failure occurred after periods of low suction pressure and elevated strainer differential pressure.

Recommended engineering actions:
Review minimum flow bypass sizing, validate NPSH margin, inspect impeller condition, confirm coupling alignment tolerance, and update preventive maintenance task list.
