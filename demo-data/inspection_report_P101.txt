# Inspection Report INSP-P101-2026-02
Date: 2026-02-20
Asset: P101 Condensate Transfer Pump
Location: Unit A Train 1
Inspector: Reliability Engineer

Finding:
Pump P101 shows repeated vibration anomaly after mechanical seal replacement. Seal flush flow was below expected range during startup. Suction strainer differential pressure was elevated and operators reported cavitation noise.

Process parameters:
Suction pressure: 1.2 bar
Discharge pressure: 8.4 bar
Vibration: 7.8 mm/s RMS
Seal flush flow: below OEM recommendation

Recommendation:
Inspect suction strainer, verify pump operating point, check coupling alignment, and review FlowServe P101 manual before next seal replacement.
