# SOP-22 Pump Isolation and Maintenance Preparation
Revision: 8
Applies to: Pump P101 and centrifugal process pumps

Before maintenance on Pump P101:
1. Issue permit-to-work.
2. Apply lockout tagout on motor starter and local disconnect.
3. Close suction and discharge isolation valves.
4. Drain casing and verify zero pressure.
5. Isolate seal flush line.
6. Verify stored energy release.
7. Confirm no hot work or line break hazards remain.

Safety hazards:
stored energy, line break, hot surface, rotating equipment, chemical exposure.

Required evidence:
LOTO checklist, zero pressure verification, permit-to-work, and technician sign-off.
