# Incident Report INC-C201-2026-01
Date: 2026-01-27
Asset: C201 Instrument Air Compressor
Location: Train 2

Incident:
Compressor C201 tripped on TRIP-OIL-LOW during plant restart. Operators observed bearing overheating and unstable discharge pressure before shutdown.

Immediate action:
Operations switched to standby compressor. Maintenance replaced collapsed oil filter element, inspected drive-end bearing, and calibrated the low oil pressure switch.

Likely causes:
oil contamination, overdue preventive maintenance interval, restricted oil filter, and insufficient post-maintenance functional testing.

RCA requirement:
Generate RCA for compressor C201 and compare with prior oil analysis records.
