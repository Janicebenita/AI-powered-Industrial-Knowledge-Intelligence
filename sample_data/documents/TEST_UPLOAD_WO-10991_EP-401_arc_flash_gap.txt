# Maintenance and Safety Work Order WO-10991
Date: 2026-06-18
Asset: EP-401 MCC Electrical Panel in Bay E-4
Location: Bay E-4
Role: safety officer and electrical technician

Inspection finding:
EP-401 was reviewed during the monthly electrical safety walkdown. The panel has an outdated arc flash label and the latest energized work procedure was not attached to the equipment record.

Alarm code:
FAULT-BKR-TRIP was reported by operations during restart.

Maintenance action:
Technician inspected feeder breaker, torqued accessible lugs, cleaned panel ventilation filter, and verified breaker trip history. No live work was performed.

Safety hazards:
Arc flash, stored energy, and energized electrical work.

Regulatory reference:
NFPA-70E requires current arc flash labeling and documented energized work controls for electrical panels.

Recommended next action:
Update arc flash study label, attach energized work SOP, and schedule follow-up inspection before the next compliance audit.
